Jaeseung Lee

jason2133@korea.ac.kr

I am a Master Student who is majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Korea University, where I received my Bachelor's Degree in Statistics and Software Technology & Entrepreneurship Program. I am working as a graduate researcher of the Multimedia Information Lab, advised by Prof. Eenjun Hwang.

I am deeply interested in methods aimed at representing and analyzing complex social phenomena using Graph Neural Networks and Large Language Models with higher accuracy and efficiency (e.g. GNN-based Multivariate Time-Series Forecasting, LLM-based Information Retrieval, and Social Network Analysis).

For the industrial application, I am interested in Educational Data Mining, which incorporates Statistical Machine Learning into Educational Tasks (e.g. Student Performance Prediction, LLM-based Question-Answering Model, and Educational Contents Recommendation).
